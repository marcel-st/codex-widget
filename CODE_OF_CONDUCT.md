# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a practical and respectful place to collaborate.
Contributors are expected to communicate clearly, stay on topic, and treat
other people with respect.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal
attacks, publishing private information, or repeatedly disrupting project work.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors
when behavior is harmful to the project or its users.
